Biography

I was born in Xining City, Qinghai Province, China, in 1997. I obtained my B.E. degrees in automation from Dept. of Artificial Intelligence, Nanjing Agricultural University in 2020, under the supervision of A/Prof. Wei Lu. I obtained my M.S. degree in electronic information with the Dept. of Unmanned system Research, Northwestern Polytechnical University in 2023, under the supervision of Prof. Yangwang Fang and A/Prof. Dengxiu Yu. From August 2024, I will be studying for a Ph.D. at the University of Macau, Department of Electromechanical Engineering, under the supervision of Dr. Jason J. R. Liu.

I am interested in networked system control theories and their applications in intelligent unmanned systems. I welcome colleagues and seniors to communicate and cooperate with me, exploring exciting research together.
